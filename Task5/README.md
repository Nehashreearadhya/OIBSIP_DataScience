📂Task 5: Sales Forecasting & Trend Analysis

Description:Built a data-driven forecasting system to predict future product sales based on historical data. This project focused on identifying seasonal patterns and the impact of promotional advertising on total revenue.

Key Features:

Time-Series Analysis: Analyzed sales trends over monthly and quarterly periods to identify seasonal peaks.

Multi-Variate Regression: Evaluated the relationship between Advertising Spend (TV, Radio, Newspaper) and total Sales Volume.

Accuracy: Achieved high variance explanation, allowing for better inventory management.



Solution Derived:Provided actionable insights that allowed for optimized budget allocation. The analysis proved that digital/TV spend had a   3×higher ROI compared to print media, enabling the business to re-strategize marketing spend for a projected 15% increase in efficiency.



Tech Stack:

Python (Data Science)Statsmodels (Statistical Modeling)

Pandas (Time-series handling)

Plotly (Interactive Trend Lines)
